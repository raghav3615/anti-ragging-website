ABOUT US

The aim of the website is to eliminate ragging in all its forms from universities and other higher educational institutions in the country. To make a ragging-free campus, this will be achieved by preventing its occurrence and punishing those who indulge in ragging, in accordance with the Supreme Court Regulations.

Statistics:

Over 50% of students have reported being ragged at least once during their university life.

Real-Life Story:

In 2019, a first-year student spoke out against ragging, leading to the expulsion of several seniors who were involved in severe mental and physical harassment.

Definition of Ragging

Any act of physical or mental abuse targeted at another student based on their religion, caste, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, appearance, nationality, regional origins, linguistic identity, place of residence, or economic background.

Aims & Objectives

To ensure a ragging-free campus by constituting an anti-ragging committee and spreading awareness through banners and stickers. To make students aware of the laws and punishments related to ragging.

Anti-Ragging Cell

Regular interaction and counseling with students to detect early signs of ragging. Conducting anti-ragging workshops, seminars, and programs with advocates and other judicial personalities.
